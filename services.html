Dental Cleansing

80% of dogs and 70% of cats over 3 years old have some form of periodontal (dental) disease. Dental diseases cause bad breath (halitosis) and pain; it is also a source of infection and can make your pet seriously ill.
Dental disease is preventable in the vast majority of cases and in most cases, easy to achieve at home. There are many different methods to keep your pet's 'pearly white' teeth and these should be started while they are still puppies and kittens.
For adult cats and dogs with existing dental disease, a dental treatment with a scale and polish under general aesthetic is ofeten necessary to get their mouth back into good condition. This will allow us to start prevention with a clean mouth which needs to be continued at home to prevent dental disease developing again in the future.
