About FurEver Pets

A Legacy of Love, Care and Committment to your Furry Friend.

Our Story

FurEver Petcare began with a deep love for animals and a simple goal — to offer pets the same care and compassion we give to our own. What started as a small grooming shop has blossomed into a trusted petcare center offering grooming, training, nutrition, and veterinary services. We believe every pet deserves to live a healthy, happy life, and every pet owner deserves peace of mind.

We treat your pets as our kids! Our goal is to treat each one of your furry companions with utmost patience and care.

Driven by Compassion, Guided by Purpose

At Furver Pets, our staff members share a burning passion & love for pets and are guaranteed to be very patient and gentle with it. We understand the personality features of each breed, as much as we ar knowledgeable about pets' skin health conditions.
We make sure your pet relaxes and has fun with us during all sessions. We are committed to enhancing the lives of pets and their families by providing loving care, trusted services and a lifelong partnership.
